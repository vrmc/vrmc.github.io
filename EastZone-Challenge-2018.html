About East Zone Robotics Challenge 2018

The East Zone Science and Technology Centre at VJC organized the 2018 East Zone Robotics Challenge as part of the centre's effort to inspire and create greater interest in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math during the newly revised STEM Festival (formerly known as Science Carnival@VJC). This event provides a platform for both primary and secondary school students to exercise their creativity and problem-solving skills in the field of Robotics. In total, we had 2 challenges, the Autonomous Challenge and the Exploration Challnege, each consisting of a heavy and a light category. Furthermore, 3 special awards, for Best in Mechanical Construction, Most Innovative Design and Most Efficient Robot, were awarded to 1 primary and 1 secondary school each.
