About NUS Hack & Roll 2019

The NUS Hack & Roll 2019 is a hackathon that takes place over the course of 2 years. NUS welcomes students and non-students to come up with a project in a team of no more than 4 members. They also welcome hardware projects, and all projects will be judged based on creativity, technical accomplishment, polish, and usefulness, in that order.

After much brainstorming and formulating of ther ideas, our team of three, consisting of Chen Hao, Rubesh and Titus, came up with this amazing project for submission.



Due to their familiarity with Arduino, they used Arduino IDE to write the codes and cme up with the AquaClock. This practical invention serves to wake people up in the morning. Unlike a regular alarm clock, the AquaClock will spray water onto the users if they fail to turn off the alarm clock in time.
