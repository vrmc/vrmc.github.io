What Is VEX?

This is the first time our VEX specialisation took part in the Singapore VEX Robotics National Championship. The competition had the same task as the 2019 VEX Robotics championship, "Tower Takeover". This allowed our team a longer duration of time to improve on our robots, as well as learn from other teams who took part in the 2019 competition.
