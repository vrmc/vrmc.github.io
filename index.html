About VRMC

In the Robotics–Makers Club, members learn specialised topics that make them masters of the world of tomorrow. We equip our members with the skills and tools to build outstanding projects and creations.

89 Ideas Sprouted
38 Projects Accomplished
3,543 Man Hours Worked
31 Hard Workers

We are a collaboration of inquisitive students that build and breathe technology

The only hub in VJ allowing students to develop interest based projects

What do we develop?

VEX Robotics

We have been specialised in VEX since the advent of this Club, and have worked in various projects involving Advanced Mechanics and Automation.

Drones

We work on Fixed-Wing Planes, FPV drones and quadcopter.

Arduino & Electronics

It involves student initiated projects which are developed with Arduino, Electronics and Raspberry Pi.
