More about VJ Street View

Due to lack of time and effort for everyone to come down to VJC during Open House or any other time, there is a lack of platform for people to know more about the facilities that entail VJC. Hence, in order for everyone to know about VJ's facilities, the street view will give them a walkthrough of VJ.



                                     


Using Google Street View as the base for the project, and with expertise in programming, the team worked day and night on this project for the benefit of VJ. This helped the school a lot as more people were able to know more about VJ's layout through this software. We are really proud of the team on their effort. Thank you.
